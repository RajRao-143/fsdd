About CodeVita

CodeVita is an international coding competition that encourages students and professionals to sharpen their problem solving and programming skills. This demo page recreates a contest portal: register, browse problems, submit solutions, and track your leaderboard standing.

FAQ

How do I submit a solution?

Write your solution in any supported language, compile and submit through the submission form. This demo does not include an online judge backend.

What languages are supported?

Common contest languages: C/C++, Java, Python, JavaScript, C#.
